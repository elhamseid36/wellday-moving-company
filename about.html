A Heritage of Seamless Transitions

Founded in 2016 on the principle of absolute client privacy and white-glove service, WELLDAY was created to fill a gap in the market for clients requiring superior logistics management for high-value and sensitive relocations.

“Our mission is not merely to move possessions, but to move lives with respect, precision, and zero stress.”

We achieve this through rigorous staff training, the use of proprietary packing materials, and a commitment to communication that keeps you informed at every step, without burdening your time.
